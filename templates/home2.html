COVID-19 Preparedness and Recovery

Maintaining Consistent IV Supply Throughout the COVID-19 Crisis and the New Normal

As the global COVID-19 pandemic continues to challenge the delivery of healthcare worldwide, ICU Medical is committed to working with our customers to ensure you have the IV solutions, systems, and consumables you need to provide essential care.

Because evolving protocols and variations in care shouldn't mean fluctuations in supply, ICU Medical is ready with a flexible, domestic supply chain to help keep you supplied throughout the COVID-19 crisis and into the new normal.
